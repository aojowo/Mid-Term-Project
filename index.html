I love Doraemon, and so I decided to create a website themed around it. My fondness for Doraemon is not just for the character itself, but for the process of directing the feature films, involving various orchestras, the changing storylines depending on the director, and the way each film maintains its connection to the original work while also introducing its unique originality. Although the original creator has passed away, the passion of the successors who strive to continue delivering the messages through the work is what I believe keeps this long-running series popular. While I enjoy both the original Doraemon comics and the animation series, my favorite is the feature film series. The most entertaining film for me has been [Doraemon the Movie: Nobita's Sky Utopia]. Below is the movie poster for that feature film.

This feature film discussed whether a civilization like Utopia could logically exist and if the realization of a Utopia could fulfill human ideals. It was a captivating work with high-quality animation and a somewhat complex theme. While I look forward to the next feature film, I anticipate a significant change in atmosphere since the director is different and, according to releases in Japan, the film targeted a younger audience, receiving many comments on its shift in focus. This diversity in appeal across different works is why I find the Doraemon series so endearing.
